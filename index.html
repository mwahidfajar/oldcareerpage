Indonesia Office

PT. Formulatrix Indonesia was established in 2006 to manufacture and develop laboratory automation hardware and software for the life science.

The company is located in the Central Java city of Salatiga, a small city with cool weather and beautiful scenery as well as the developing city of Semarang. Formulatrix has both a manufacturing and engineering division developing advanced automation and robotics. Over the years we have expanded our office space to multiples sites with over 100.000 square feet for engineering and manufacturing space. The company continues to experience aggressive growth with healthy profits because of our industry recognized patented technology and superior software delivery services. We maintain a small-company culture with a worldwide outlook, with offices in the United States, Europe, and Asia.

We are seeking to hire people who are passionate about their work and enjoy doing it. We are rapidly expanding our employee base. We currently have more than 300 full-time employees on staff and we are seeking even more talented individuals. Please consider joining a fun place to work with a chance to challenge and grow yourself. See our available opportunities below.
